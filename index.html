Objective

The objective of the game is simple. The first player who scores 50 points wins a round. The game ends when a player wins 3 rounds.

How to Score Points

Each green sweet gives you one point while each red sweet gives your OPPONENT 5 points (wait whuttt...)

The Catch

Eating a red sweet makes your opponent longer AND slower (so you can go ahead and eat more green sweets to score points)

Moving

Use the W, A, S, D or the directional arrow keys to move the snakes

Avoid hitting yourself or your opponent!
